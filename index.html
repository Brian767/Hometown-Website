Top three activities to do at Durango

Paseo del Viejo Oeste

Actors stage a variety of entertainment on the site of a Western movie set.

Plaza de Armas

A vibrant colonial square featuring historic architecture, elegant fountains, and a central bandstand.

Mirador de los Remedios

Scenic hilltop viewpoint offering panoramic city vistas, accessible via an aerial tramway or walking.
